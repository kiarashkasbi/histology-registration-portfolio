# Biomedical Image Analysis Portfolio

This repository is a public-facing learning and application portfolio. It describes my general research direction and technical interests in computational pathology, whole-slide image analysis, image registration, and biomedical visualization.

It does not include raw thesis data, unpublished results, private implementation details, patient-level information, or project-specific material from my ongoing MSc thesis.

## Research Direction

I am an MSc Medical Biotechnology student at the University of Padua, moving from a biology-first background toward computational biomedical research. My interests are strongest where tissue biology, medical images, computational workflows, and visual quality control meet.

The public material here stays intentionally general. It is meant to show my direction and learning path, not to release thesis data or claim a finished method.

## Areas Of Interest

- Computational pathology and digital pathology
- Whole-slide image analysis
- Biomedical image registration
- Tissue visualization and 3D biomedical visualization
- Cancer bioinformatics and medical AI
- Spatial biology and image-based biomedical research
